Anime(ted) Gifs #19 – Sword Art Online II

October 2, 2015

I’ve created another huge gallery of Anime(ted) Gifs. This time I have 24 Sword Art Online II Gifs. Major Spoilers!

Sword Art Online II is the sequel to the incredibly popular Sword Art Online anime. Most would say it’s not as good as the first, but it’s not incredibly horrible. I’d say it’s mediocre. There are MAJOR SPOILERS in the gifs below. If you haven’t seen the anime, do not scroll down! Like usual, I created all of these animated gifs myself.
